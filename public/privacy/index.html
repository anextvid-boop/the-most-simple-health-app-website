Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Offline-First Data Model

The Most Simple Health App is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy. All data generated or logged within the application is strictly offline-first. Your health markers, hydration progress, nutrition data, and sleep records are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults and local binary storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Apple HealthKit Integration

We read and write data to Apple HealthKit strictly with your explicit permission to synchronize your daily logs. We do not collect, monitor, monetize, or transmit your HealthKit data to any external servers or third-party tracking services. The permissions you grant are used solely to reflect your localized health metrics within the native Apple Ecosystem.

3. Third-Party Analytics

There are zero third-party tracking SDKs, marketing payloads, or analytics frameworks embedded in the application. What you do in the app stays exclusively on your phone.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because everything is stored locally on your device, deleting the application will instantly and permanently erase all the specific data created within the app container. If you have synchronized data to Apple Health, you may individually delete that data via the centralized Apple Health App settings.

5. Contact Protocol

If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding our localized data handling practices, reach out to us directly.
